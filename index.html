About

Our inspiration for TOHacks 2021 came with our realization that many subtle joys of life are ignored because we have grown overstimulated, and thus rarely satisfied with our day to day lives. We look foward to vacations or parties, so when that is no longer an option, we feel morose and fatigued. Instead, we should pay attention to the chirping birds, the warm sunlight; peer into the puddles on the street, and see another world beneath the shimmering ripples. So start smiling, and you too will look at life from a new perspective, and notice these minute, but beautiful details. :)
